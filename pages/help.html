Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Here are some common questions and answers to help you get started:

  • Q: How do I login to the system?
    A: Navigate to the Home page section and click on the Login button. Fill in the required details and login.If you don't have an account then you can click on signup.
  • Q: How can I change my notification settings?
    A: Go to the Settings section and click on Notification Preferences. Here, you can customize how and when you receive alerts.
  • Q: What should I do if I encounter a technical issue?
    A: First, check the Troubleshooting Tips below. If the issue persists, contact support using the details provided in the Contact Support section.

Troubleshooting Tips

  • Sensor not connecting: Ensure the sensor is properly powered and within range of the Raspberry Pi. Restart the sensor and the Raspberry Pi if necessary.
  • No data is displaying: Check the internet connection and make sure all devices are connected. Refresh the dashboard or check your network settings.
  • Alert not sending: Verify that your email settings are correctly configured in the Settings section. Also, check your spam folder.
  • System running slow: Clear your browser cache and cookies. Ensure your Raspberry Pi and other devices are not running too many processes simultaneously.
